About Steel Masters NYC — Brooklyn's Steel Fabricator Since 1972

Steel Masters NYC has been Brooklyn's trusted steel fabricator since 1972, building a reputation for precision metalwork that has lasted more than five decades. Founded on the principle that quality fabrication and honest service go hand in hand, the company has grown from a local Brooklyn shop into a full-service steel fabrication operation serving Manhattan, Brooklyn, Queens, and the Bronx. Every project that leaves the Steel Masters NYC shop is built to the exact specifications agreed upon with the client, fabricated from appropriate-grade steel, and installed by experienced crews who understand New York City's exacting installation requirements. Brooklyn property owners — from individual homeowners in East New York to commercial building managers in Crown Heights — have relied on Steel Masters NYC for cellar doors, steel staircases, Economaster trash bin enclosures, and structural steel alterations that stand the test of time.
